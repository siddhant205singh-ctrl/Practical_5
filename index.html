How to Build Good Study Habits

Building strong study habits is the foundation of academic success. First, establish routine and structure.
Create a consistent study schedule that blocks out specific times for learning and review, treating these sessions like mandatory appointments.
Use the Pomodoro Technique (25 minutes of focused work, 5-minute break) to maintain concentration.
Crucially, practice Spaced Repetition, where you review material over increasing intervals rather than cramming, which solidifies long-term memory.
